Poor Navigation

Module 03 Discussion Example

This example is meant to describe the poor applicaiton of a navigational bar.

Although a navigational bar is still present, it has been poorly located within the screen. From a users perspective, they would need to search the application in order to find what they are looking for, instead of it being anchored at the top or left side of the screen. By being positioned on the left of the window, horizontal scrolling may be required to locate all of the links within this navigational bar.
